Our Story

Montgomery's Soul Food Institution

Tucked away on Early Street in west Montgomery, Barbara Gail Neighborhood Grille has earned a legendary reputation for its authentic Southern breakfast cooking. People drive across town — and across the state — just for a taste.

Every plate comes out hot, generous, and seasoned to perfection. Whether you're craving catfish with cheese grits, salmon croquettes, shrimp and grits, or crispy turkey wings — Barbara Gail delivers the real thing every single time.

And don't leave without trying the homemade Kool-Aid. Every flavor. Made fresh. Every morning. It's legendary for a reason.
