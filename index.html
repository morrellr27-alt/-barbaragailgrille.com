Our Story

A True Neighborhood Gem

Tucked away on Early Street in the heart of Montgomery, Barbara Gail Neighborhood Grille has become a beloved institution for locals and visitors alike. From the first bite, you'll understand why people drive across town — and across the state — just to eat here.

Every dish is cooked like it's coming out of your grandmother's kitchen. Hot, fresh, perfectly seasoned, and served with a smile. Whether you're craving catfish with cheese grits, salmon croquettes, or a classic breakfast sandwich, Barbara Gail delivers every single time.

Don't forget the homemade Kool-Aid — it's legendary for a reason.
